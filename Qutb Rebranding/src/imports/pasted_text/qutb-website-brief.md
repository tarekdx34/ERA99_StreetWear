# QUTB — Complete Website Rebuild

You are a world-class fashion brand creative director, UX designer, art director, and frontend engineer.

Design and build a premium fashion e-commerce website for a clothing brand called QUTB.

Do NOT create a generic Shopify fashion store.

The goal is to create the most distinctive Egyptian premium basics brand on the internet.

## Brand Positioning

QUTB is a premium basics label born in Alexandria, Egypt.

The brand is founded by a new generation that grew up inside the Egyptian garment industry through a father with decades of manufacturing experience.

The brand starts with:

* Boxy tees
* Ringer tees
* Regular tees

The obsession is:

* Fabric
* Fit
* Construction
* Longevity

The philosophy:

FABRIC FIRST. LOGO SECOND. ALWAYS.

The brand is not luxury streetwear.

The brand is not fast fashion.

The brand is not graphic-heavy.

The brand is modern Mediterranean essentials.

Think:

* Salt air
* Cotton
* Wind
* Stone
* Sunlight
* Alexandria
* Simplicity
* Confidence

## Visual Direction

The website should feel:

Mediterranean + Industrial + Editorial

Avoid:

* Neon colors
* Streetwear clichés
* Oversized animations
* Aggressive sales banners
* Popups on arrival
* Trendy startup aesthetics

The experience should feel calm, premium and timeless.

## 1. Alexandria As A Character

Alexandria should appear throughout the site without becoming tourism marketing.

Use imagery and storytelling inspired by:

* Mediterranean coastlines
* Sea rocks
* Salt formations
* Wind-blown curtains
* Old textile factories
* Workshop details
* Concrete sea barriers
* Weathered architecture

Alexandria is not a location.

Alexandria is a character.

---

## 2. Cotton Journey Section

Create an immersive section explaining:

Cotton
↓
Knitting
↓
Dyeing
↓
Cutting
↓
Sewing
↓
Alexandria
↓
Your Wardrobe

This should feel educational and beautiful.

Not corporate.

---

## 3. The Uniform Collection

Do NOT organize products as seasonal drops.

Create:

THE UNIFORM

Containing:

* Regular Tee
* Box Tee
* Ringer Tee

Permanent products.

Timeless essentials.

Only colors evolve.

This section should become a core brand asset.

---

## 4. Alexandria Product Naming

Products should use subtle Alexandria-inspired names.

Examples:

Stanley Box Tee

Montaza Ringer

Corniche Regular Tee

Bahary Heavy Tee

Never make this feel forced.

Keep it elegant.

---

## 5. Salt Journal

Create a dedicated editorial section called:

SALT JOURNAL

Content categories:

* Fabric education
* Egyptian cotton
* Fit guides
* Manufacturing stories
* Alexandria culture
* Product development
* Design philosophy

The journal should feel like a luxury magazine.

---

## 6. Packaging Philosophy

Integrate packaging storytelling throughout the website.

Possible messages:

"Made by people who grew up around fabric."

"From Alexandria. Built to stay."

"Fabric first. Always."

The packaging should feel collectible and thoughtful.

---

## 7. Bunny Symbol System

Do NOT use cute rabbits.

Do NOT use mascots.

Do NOT use cartoon graphics.

The bunny should exist only as an abstract symbol.

Possible inspiration:

Minimal rabbit ears.

A symbol that looks elegant even when people do not immediately recognize it as a bunny.

The symbol should work:

* Embroidered
* Printed
* On neck labels
* On packaging
* As favicon
* As app icon
